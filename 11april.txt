 5 Uses of Face/Object Detection
 
1. Security & Surveillance
Detect unknown people in restricted areas
Used in offices, airports, banks
Can trigger alerts automatically

2. Smart Attendance System
Recognizes faces of students/employees
Marks attendance automatically
Saves time and avoids proxy attendance

3. Self-Driving Cars
Detects pedestrians, vehicles, traffic signals
Helps avoid accidents
Used in autonomous driving systems

4. Retail & Customer Analytics
Detects customer movement in stores
Identifies popular products/areas
Helps improve store layout

5. Mobile Apps & Filters
Face filters (like Snapchat/Instagram)
Face unlock features in phones
Augmented reality applications
